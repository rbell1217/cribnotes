Why CribNotes

Built for the moments that matter

A childcare app shouldn't make the sitter scroll through a wall of text while a toddler tugs at their leg. CribNotes shows the right thing at the right time.

🎙️

Dictate, don't type

Parents talk through the day naturally. CribNotes transcribes, cleans, and auto-files instructions into the right sections with context tags.

🛡️

Critical info, always pinned

Allergies, current medications, insurance, blood type, and pediatrician live at the top of every guide. Never context-filtered, never buried.

📍

Quick statuses, one tap

"Down for nap." "Just ate." "Diaper change." The sitter logs it in a tap. Parents get the chronological feed in real time.

🚩

Flag a moment, send a push

Something the parent should know now? Sitter flags the entry, parent gets an instant push notification. No SMS thread chaos.

📶

Works in the basement

Service worker + offline persistence keep the guide readable when the basement playroom has bad signal. Syncs when reconnected.

🤝

Shift-to-shift handoffs

Multiple sitters? Each shift leaves a note for the next. The day-of-the-week filter respects whoever is on the clock.
